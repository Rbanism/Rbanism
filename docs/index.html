we want to build digital competence and skills for:

Reproducibility
Reproducibility is essential in ensuring that results can be easily verified and replicated. It facilitates the sharing of analyses and solutions, promoting transparency and collaboration.

Automation
Automation plays a crucial role in the fields of urban planning and programming with the R language. It reduces manual effort, enhances efficiency, and enables the rapid execution of complex tasks.

Scalability
Scalability is vital for handling large datasets and addressing complex urban challenges. It enables the application of methods and models at the urban or regional scale, leading to more informed and sustainable decision-making.
